Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Jarvis personal assistant application ("Jarvis," "we," "our") handles information in connection with its SMS messaging features. Jarvis is a personal-use application operated by an individual sole proprietor for their own use only.

Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

Jarvis processes the following information solely for the purpose of operating the personal assistant:

  • The mobile phone number of the account owner (the sole recipient of messages).
  • Message content sent to and from the account owner.
  • Timestamps and delivery metadata required to operate the messaging service.

How Information Is Used

Information is used exclusively to deliver messages between the account owner and the Jarvis application. No information is used for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose any information to third parties for marketing or any other purpose. The only third party involved is Twilio, which provides the underlying messaging infrastructure and processes messages strictly to deliver them. Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Message data is retained only as long as needed for the application to function and is deleted thereafter.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect data, including encrypted transport and access controls on the self-hosted application.

Your Rights

As the sole user, the account owner may delete any stored data at any time by accessing the self-hosted application directly.
